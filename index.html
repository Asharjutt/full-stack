Safety Guidelines & Travel Restrictions

Important Safety Protocols

  • Wear masks at all times inside the airport.
  • Sanitize hands regularly using provided hand sanitizers.
  • Maintain social distancing (6 feet) in all areas.
  • Follow temperature checks and health screenings before boarding.
  • Stay home if you're feeling sick or have COVID-19 symptoms.

Travel Restrictions

  • Check travel restrictions for your destination country.
  • Some countries may require a negative COVID test result.
  • Quarantine may be required upon arrival in some countries.
  • International travelers must complete health declarations.
  • Stay updated on flight cancellations or delays due to travel bans.
